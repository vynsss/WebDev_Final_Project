She is the first to view a customer order, and organises all of the staff, times and resources to ensure that everything is done to meet the order or inquiry request. Cheni is an experienced product export manager, has an excellent understanding of the Bali market place, and keeps in touch with all our important vendors to ensure we always are able to provide the best products, prices and manufacturing services for you.......

Young, energetic and always in for a good laugh, Viko is the point person for all customer communication.Viko represents Bali Products during most communications with customers, and make sure all your questions, quotes and queries are answered, and followed up on time. For urgent matters she is also available for video conversations to clarify and resolve any issues.......

If you have been to Bali before, you know that dealing with manufacturing vendors can be a challenge at times! Not for Ruly. Ruly is a very experienced and knowledgeable customer liaison officer, whose main role is to accompany overseas guests on sourcing tours. He is an expert in understanding and defining your product requirements, and then negotiating and communicating directly with our manufacturing vendors to make sure your product needs, designs and specifications are met. He will also be your guide during sourcing tours to find the best solutions for your needs.......

Koming is our cheerful accountant, who is in charge of office entertainment, and tracking and making payments to all our vendors, in order to ensure products are available when required. Koming knows the company's spread sheets like the back of her hand and always makes sure the figures match up at the end of the day.......

Ame is the head of marketing, and is the one who is in charge of online technology. He makes sure we use state-of-the-art internet technology and stay on top of the ever-changing internet world. He manages and markets our sourcing services and our online shop, to ensure the best shopping, buying and service experience for our customers.......
