Looking for Bali style interior design services & products?

Building a new hotel or villa in Bali, and need help designing a Bali style interior to meet your needs? Or selling Bali villas and want to offer your clients a number of soft furniture packages to choose from? No matter what you interior needs, Bali Products can provide an interior solution for you.
What we do

Bali Products provides Bali Hotel & Bali Villas property developers, builders, and individual property owners with a one-stop Bali style interior design, interior fit-out, and interior furnishing packages solution. Bali Products customers can select from a comprehensive range of Bali style interior solutions, including Customized Interior Design; an Interior Fit-Out Service; a set of Interior Furnishing Packages; as well as an Interiors Sourcing & Buying Service, to find and select luxury Bali & Indonesian Style furniture and furnishing collections, from contemporary to antique styles.

No matter if your interior needs are for a single residence, a hotel or villa complex, or a commercial office, Bali Products provides a total interior solution, tailored to meet your budget and style.

Customized interior designs

We work with individual homeowners, renovators, builders, and property developers building Hotel & Villa estate style or Villa complex developments, with a complete customized interior design service, both at the Pre-Construction stage, and Post-Construction stage of their development:

- Pre-construction project services

We work with Architects, Renovators, Property Developers and Building Designers to develop a Bali Interior Design Theme, during the initial building design stage, on a project basis.

We work from the initial plans, and provide input into room size, layouts, room specifications, room locations etc. to best match the project style and project budget of the owner.

We can also work with owners and architects pre-project to provide pre-project costing’s in regard to interior layout, materials etc. in order to assist owners to develop an interior design project budget.

To find out more about the interior designing services that we offer property developers seeking Bali style interiors, please take a look at our Balinese interior Design page.

- Post-construction project services

We also work with home owners, property developers and builders post building construction. After the building framework has been completed, we work with builders, owners and project managers to design all the interior spaces, in regards to interior materials, colors, accessories etc.

To find out more about the interior designing services that we offer property developers seeking Bali style interiors, please take a look at our Balinese Interior Design page.
