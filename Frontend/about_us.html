About Us

Bali Products brings the very best retail and wholesale products, household furnishings, fashion, home accessories and gifts from Bali and Indonesia, and gives them to the world. We provide overseas shoppers, interior designers, importers and wholesale buyers searching for finished or made-to-order luxury accessory products and furnishings from Bali, with a comprehensive range of online Private Wholesale Catalogs, and an integrated set of on-island Sourcing and Buying Agency Services.

Who uses our services? Just about anyone seeking home, showroom, hotel and resort products, furnishings and accessories from Bali & Indonesia. We cater for private buyers and shoppers sourcing select finished accessory products and furnishings from Bali and Indonesia for their personal use; commercial wholesalers, importers and retailers wanting to purchase interior luxury furnishings, furniture ranges and product accessories from Bali for wholesale or retail outlets, chains, stores and shops; home ware, furnishings and fashion accessory suppliers and owners seeking private/white label products and made-to-order product ranges; and overseas (or on-island) interior designers & decorators fitting out luxury homes, hotels, villas and resort projects.

Our clients have access to our own comprehensive and up to date Private Wholesale Catalogs, which include a Bali Furniture Catalog, a Bali Silver Jewelry Catalog, a Bali Jewelry (Non-Silver) Catalog, a Bali Beads Catalog, a Bali Wood Carving Catalog, a Bali Art Catalog, a Bali Handicrafts Catalog, a Bali Sarongs Catalog, and a Bali Clothing & Fashion Accessories Decalogue. And if it can’t be found in our own catalogs, don’t worry…..we will either source it for you, design it with you, and/or arrange to have it made by one of our quality vendors.
